Clinical Helpdesk

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about scheduling, invoices, prescriptions, or emergency services? Read answers to common patient questions below.

How do I book an appointment online?
You can schedule visits by navigating to the Appointments section. Select your specialty clinical department (e.g. Cardiology, Orthopedics), assign an available doctor, check a calendar date, pick a time slot, and submit the booking form.
Do you support health insurance packages?
Yes, CareGrid integrates with leading medical insurance groups. When generating invoices inside the Billing Panel, you can select 'Insurance Coverage' as the payment method to compute policy deductible rates.
How can I contact trauma ambulance services?
Our emergency ambulance line is active 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can reach our critical triage hotline immediately at +1 (555) 911-CARE for dispatch coordination.
What are the general visiting hours?
Outpatient ward visits are allowed between 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM daily. For Intensive Care Units (ICU), visiting hours are restricted to 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM, subject to physician clearance.
How do I download diagnostic reports and prescriptions?
Active medical prescriptions can be viewed inside the Prescriptions page. Each record contains print and PDF download buttons to keep physical copies of dosage directives.
What payment methods do you support?
We support standard billing channels including Cash payment, Credit/Debit card swipes, UPI digital transfers, and direct health insurance claims processing.
